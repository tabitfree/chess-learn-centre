Sicilian Defense

The most commonly used counter-move to White's 1.e4 is the Sicilian Defense. This opening is well-regarded and strategically sound, and is utilized by both novice and expert chess players. However, the Sicilian is an aggressive opening that often results in dynamic and intense game positions.
